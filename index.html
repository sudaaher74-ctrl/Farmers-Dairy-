About Our Dairy Farm

Our story, values, and commitment to quality

Our Story

Fresh Meadows Dairy was established in 2005 with a simple mission: to provide fresh, high-quality dairy products directly from our farm to your table. What started as a small family-run farm has grown into a trusted local dairy business serving the Navi Mumbai community.

Our Commitment

We are committed to sustainable farming practices, animal welfare, and delivering the freshest products. Our cows are grass-fed, free-range, and never treated with artificial hormones. We believe in transparency and take pride in our farming methods.

Why Choose Us?

  • 100% organic and natural products
  • Same-day harvesting and delivery
  • No artificial preservatives or additives
  • Hygienic processing and packaging
  • Supporting local farmers and economy
  • Environmentally sustainable practices
